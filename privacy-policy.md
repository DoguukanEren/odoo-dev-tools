# Privacy Policy — Odoo Dev Tools

**Last updated: March 2026**

## Data Collection

Odoo Dev Tools does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

## How It Works

All operations are performed **locally in your browser** on the active Odoo page:

- Field and model information is fetched directly from your Odoo instance via its own JSON-RPC API
- RPC call monitoring is done in-memory and never leaves your browser
- Panel position and preferences are stored locally using `chrome.storage.local` on your device only

## Third Parties

No data is shared with any third party. No analytics, no tracking, no remote logging.
